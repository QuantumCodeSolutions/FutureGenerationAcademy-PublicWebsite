About Us

Here at Clayhall Nursery we live by our motto “….preparing for life…” The fast-paced modern world, the prevalence of social media and competition for schools and jobs, means there are new challenges and pressures faced by us all in this time, especially our children. Our mission is to provide an additional foundation away from the home, where your child will be brimming with self-confidence and also have assurance in their own abilities. We do this through the skills of our dedicated experienced team, where we follow the early years foundation stage curriculum, but also add our own innovative ways to learn.

Clayhall Nursery and Pre-School is available for children 3 months to 5 years old.

Opening hours are:

Monday to Friday

8am to 6pm

(excluding Christmas week and bank holidays)

Places are available for full days and half days.

We currently support the free 15 hours for 3 + year olds, please contact us as info@clayhallnursery.co.uk for more information.

More about us:

Clayhall Nursery and Pre-School is situated in Clayhall and is easily accessible from Barkingside, Gants Hill, Redbridge, Ilford and South Woodford.

We offer full and part time professional childcare and pre-school education for children from 3 months to 5 years old. We are open between 8.00am to 6.00pm Monday to Friday. We are closed for Christmas week and bank holidays.

Our Nursery is situated over two floors, providing a spacious, happy and relaxed atmosphere throughout. The Nursery has been designed for comfort and the safety of all children, and is registered with Ofsted (Office for standards in education). It is regularly inspected and we are a member of the Early Years Development and Childcare Partnership. The staff are encouraged to attend regular training and we are kept up to date with all new childcare areas.

Outside the Nursery there is a securely fenced hard and soft play area, which is used daily for all age groups.
