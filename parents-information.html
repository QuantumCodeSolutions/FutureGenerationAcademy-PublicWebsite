Parent's Information

We look forward to meeting you

We hope you choose Clayhall Nursery and Pre-School for your childcare. We are endeavouring to become a community focal point for local parents and assist you in your busy lives by giving you peace of mind that your child is in a safe, happy, loving environment.

Your family will be part of our extended Clayhall family, where we will listen to your feedback as we continually improve our services in line with the early years curriculum. We look forward to meeting you soon!

Settling in

When your child starts at Clayhall Nursery and Pre-School, the settling in period is handled with the most loving care for your child and respect for their parents wishes. We are very flexible in our approach as we recognise that each child is different. We will ensure your child is settled in to ensure they feel comfortable and safe, plus ensuring parents are fully at ease.

Prior to starting, we have a settling in procedure. On the first day we ask that the parent stay with the child for a 2-hour period either 10am to 12pm or 2pm to 4pm. This allows the child’s relationship with the carers and other children in the room to develop and gives us an opportunity to learn your child’s routine and preferences.
